**深度学习发展历程与现状**
**Development History and Current Status of Deep Learning**

其发展最早可追溯至二十世纪四十年代，W. S. McCulloch与W. Pitts试图模拟人脑在处理信息时的神经反应，设计了一种称为MCP的简化人工神经元模型，其基本涵盖了如今单一神经元具备的核心功能：权重参数、加权求和与激活函数。在此基础上，Frank Rosenblatt于1958年提出了感知机Perceptron模型，一种基于MCP神经元的前馈人工神经网络结构。通过对输入特征向量进行线性加权求和，并结合阈值函数实现分类。然而，感知机Perceptron被证实其形成的线性分隔平面无法解决异或问题。1982年，John J. Hopfield设计并提出霍普菲尔德网络Hopfield Network。由于将每个神经元的输出信号连接至其他神经元的输入端，因此被认为是最早的全连接型递归神经网络（Recurrent Neural Network, RNN）。与此同时，反向传播（Backpropagation, BP）算法作为深度学习的关键基础算法，于1986年由Geoffrey E. Hinton等人在《Learning internal representations by error-propagation》文献中提出。其算法原理涉及：信号特征从输入层向输出层的前向传播、基于标签误差的反向链式求导以及通过梯度下降法对人工神经网络参数的全局更新。与早期感知机Perceptron模型不同，反向传播算法通过明确的误差反向传播机制，有效解决了多层感知机（Multilayer Perceptron, MLP）训练问题，提升了神经网络的非线性表征能力，而这也是现代深度学习算法体系的里程碑级成果，为后续的深度神经网络奠定基础。此外，Elman网络与LeNet网络也作为深度学习代表成果。前者由Jeffrey Elman于1990年提出，一种具有记忆单元、反馈连接与多层结构的前馈网络，主要用于本文、语音等序列数据；后者则成为第一种卷积神经网络（Convolutional Neural Networks, CNN）由Yann LeCun于1998年提出。受限于当时的数据与算力条件，LeNet网络仍能成功识别手写体。1997年，Sepp Hochreiter提出了长短时记忆（Long Short-Term Memory, LSTM）网络，在一定程度上缓解了长输入序列中信息遗忘与前后依赖问题，成为面向处理时序性数据的经典模型。其中，LSTM通过预先设定的多种门控单元，使其能够保留长序列数据的历史信息。2003年，Yoshua Bengio提出了神经网络语言模型（Neural Network Language Model, NNLM），首次将神经网络引入自然语言处理（Natural Language Processing, NLP），通过连续向量空间映射表征离散词符的“词嵌入”思想，从概率建模视角缓解了维度灾难与稀疏问题，被视为从统计语言模型迈向表征语言模型的转折点。

The development of deep learning can be traced back to the 1940s. In an effort to simulate the neural responses of the human brain during information processing, W. S. McCulloch and W. Pitts proposed a simplified artificial neuron model known as the MCP neuron. This model incorporated the fundamental components of modern artificial neurons, including weighted connections, weighted summation, and activation functions. Building upon this foundation, Frank Rosenblatt introduced the Perceptron model in 1958, a feedforward artificial neural network architecture based on MCP neurons. The Perceptron performed classification by linearly combining input feature vectors and applying a threshold activation function. However, it was later proven that the linear decision boundary generated by the Perceptron could not solve the exclusive-or (XOR) problem. In 1982, John J. Hopfield proposed the Hopfield Network, in which the output of each neuron is connected to the inputs of other neurons. Owing to its fully interconnected recurrent structure, it is widely regarded as one of the earliest recurrent neural networks (RNNs). Meanwhile, the Backpropagation (BP) algorithm, a cornerstone of modern deep learning, was introduced by Geoffrey E. Hinton and colleagues in 1986 through their seminal work, *Learning Internal Representations by Error Propagation*. The algorithm consists of forward signal propagation from the input layer to the output layer, backward error propagation based on the chain rule of differentiation, and global parameter updates using gradient descent optimization. Unlike the early Perceptron model, the BP algorithm provided an explicit mechanism for propagating errors through multiple network layers, effectively enabling the training of Multilayer Perceptrons (MLPs) and significantly enhancing the nonlinear representation capability of neural networks. This milestone laid the theoretical and methodological foundation for subsequent deep neural network architectures. In addition, the Elman Network and LeNet Network represent two influential milestones in the development of deep learning. Proposed by Jeffrey Elman in 1990, the Elman Network is a feedforward neural network equipped with memory units, feedback connections, and a multilayer structure, making it particularly suitable for processing sequential data such as text and speech. In contrast, the LeNet Network, proposed by Yann LeCun in 1998, is widely recognized as the first successful Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). Despite the limitations of available datasets and computational resources at the time, LeNet demonstrated remarkable performance in handwritten digit recognition tasks. In 1997, Sepp Hochreiter proposed the Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) network, which alleviated, to a certain extent, the problems of information loss and long-range dependency in lengthy input sequences. Consequently, LSTM became one of the most representative models for sequential data processing. Through a set of predefined gating mechanisms, LSTM is capable of selectively retaining and updating historical information over long time horizons. Subsequently, in 2003, Yoshua Bengio proposed the Neural Network Language Model (NNLM), marking the first successful introduction of neural networks into Natural Language Processing (NLP). By mapping discrete words into continuous vector spaces, NNLM pioneered the concept of word embeddings, which mitigated the curse of dimensionality and data sparsity from a probabilistic modeling perspective. This work is widely regarded as a turning point from traditional statistical language models toward representation-based language modeling.

近年来，众多以深度学习为基础的代表性成果相继涌现，特别是由美国OpenAI人工智能研究公司于2018年开发基于Transformer架构的生成式预训练语言模型（Generative Pre-trained Transformer, GPT）与2022年发布经对话数据集微调的ChatGPT，由此掀起了关于大语言模型（Large Language Model, LLM）的研究热潮，带动了各领域对人工智能技术的推广应用。然而，根据时间轴可知，在2012年由Alex Krizhevsky、Ilya Sutskever以及Geoffrey E. Hinton提出AlexNet网络并且开启“深度学习时代”之前，经历了约10年的沉积期。同时，除了人工神经网络、反向传播算法与梯度下降法之外，作为深度学习“三要素”的数据与算力也开始取得实质性发展。其中，以美国斯坦福Stanford大学Feifei Li教授提出的ImageNet数据集以及Jensen Huang等人创办的美国NVIDIA公司所推出的图形处理单元（Graphics processing unit, GPU）为代表。

In recent years, numerous landmark achievements based on deep learning have emerged. Among them, the Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT), developed by the American artificial intelligence research company [OpenAI](https://openai.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com) in 2018 and built upon the Transformer architecture, as well as ChatGPT, which was released in 2022 and fine-tuned using large-scale conversational datasets, have sparked unprecedented interest in Large Language Models (LLMs). These advancements have significantly accelerated the adoption and application of artificial intelligence technologies across a wide range of domains. However, as illustrated in the timeline, prior to the introduction of AlexNet by Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, and Geoffrey E. Hinton in 2012—which is widely regarded as the beginning of the “Deep Learning Era”—the field experienced an approximately decade-long period of relatively slow progress. During this period, although artificial neural networks, the backpropagation algorithm, and gradient-based optimization methods had already been established, the other two essential elements of deep learning, namely data and computational power, had yet to reach the level required for large-scale practical deployment. Subsequently, substantial breakthroughs were achieved in both aspects. Representative examples include the ImageNet dataset proposed by Fei-Fei Li at Stanford University, which provided a large-scale benchmark for visual recognition tasks, and the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) technology developed by [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com), a company co-founded by Jensen Huang. The availability of massive datasets and high-performance parallel computing hardware significantly enhanced the training efficiency of deep neural networks and ultimately laid the foundation for the rapid advancement of modern deep learning.

对于数据而言，ImageNet项目作为用于视觉对象识别的大型可视化数据库，包含超过20000个类别和1400万张标注图像，ImageNet大规模视觉识别挑战赛也成为计算机视觉（Computer Vision, CV）领域的知名竞赛之一。在竞赛机制推动下，众多里程碑式深度神经网络结构不断涌现，包括了AlexNet、ZFNet、VGG、GoogLeNet与ResNet等历年冠亚军级经典模型。在2012年，作为冠军的AlexNet网络由Alex Krizhevsky、Ilya Sutskever与Geoffrey E. Hinton共同提出。其核心贡献是引入了由卷积层和全连接层组成的神经网络结构；采用了ReLU作为激活函数；提出了随机失活Dropout缓解过拟合；使用了基于动量的小批量梯度下降加速收敛；采用了数据增强降低过拟合风险；利用了NVIDIA GeForce GTX 580实现并行计算后大幅加速网络模型的训练。因此，2012年被认为是深度学习爆发的时间点。2013年，冠军网络ZFNet在AlexNet的基础上对卷积核尺寸、数量与步长进行了调整。2014年，冠军网络GoogLeNet和亚军网络VGG均对卷积结构做出创新。前者GoogLeNet提出Inception架构，通过在同一层并行引入不同尺度的卷积核，有效增强多尺度建模能力。同时，GoogLeNet去除了AlexNet中参数量占比极高的前两层全连接，采用平均池化使得参数量降至AlexNet的十二分之一。在训练方面，GoogLeNet在中间层引入辅助分类器，通过额外的监督信号缓解了深层网络的梯度消失问题，提高了模型训练的稳定性与收敛效率。后者VGG网络由牛津大学的视觉几何组提出，在摒弃AlexNet中11×11与5×5的大型卷积核后，转而采用多个3×3尺寸的小卷积核扩大感受野。此外，VGG去除了AlexNet网络的局部响应归一化运算操作。通常而言，更深层的神经网络能提取更高级的特征。但随着网络规模与参数总量的增加，一方面，在反向传播中容易引发梯度消失或梯度爆炸问题，导致模型收敛速度变慢甚至崩塌；另一方面，数据拟合能力将更容易捕获噪声特征，进而增加过拟合风险，削弱了模型对未知数据分布的泛化性。2015年，冠军网络ResNet由微软亚洲研究院的Kaiming He提出，该网络创新性地引入残差连接方式后，解决了深层神经网络所面临的反向梯度传播困难等挑战。其核心思想是采用“跳跃连接”方式使得输入能与输出叠加，从而在反向传播时保留原始信息并促进梯度流动。此外，ResNet在结构中系统性引入了批量归一化Batch Normalization以缓解梯度消失与梯度爆炸问题。现如今，基于ResNet网络的残差结构已成为大型深度神经网络的必需架构之一。

With respect to data, the ImageNet project has become one of the most influential large-scale visual databases for object recognition research. It contains more than 20,000 categories and over 14 million annotated images. Furthermore, the ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge (ILSVRC) has evolved into one of the most prestigious competitions in the field of Computer Vision (CV). Driven by the competitive mechanism of ILSVRC, numerous milestone deep neural network architectures have emerged, including AlexNet, ZFNet, VGG, GoogLeNet, and ResNet, many of which achieved championship or runner-up positions in successive competitions. In 2012, the winning AlexNet architecture was proposed by Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, and Geoffrey E. Hinton. Its major contributions included the introduction of a deep neural network architecture composed of convolutional layers and fully connected layers, the adoption of the Rectified Linear Unit (ReLU) activation function, the proposal of the Dropout technique to mitigate overfitting, the use of mini-batch gradient descent with momentum to accelerate convergence, the application of data augmentation to improve generalization performance, and the utilization of NVIDIA GeForce GTX 580 GPUs for parallel computing, which significantly accelerated model training. Consequently, 2012 is widely regarded as the beginning of the modern deep learning revolution. In 2013, the winning ZFNet architecture further improved upon AlexNet by refining the convolutional kernel sizes, numbers of filters, and stride settings. In 2014, both the championship-winning GoogLeNet and the runner-up VGG network introduced significant innovations in convolutional network design. Specifically, GoogLeNet proposed the Inception architecture, which enhanced multi-scale feature extraction by incorporating convolutional kernels of different sizes in parallel within the same layer. Moreover, GoogLeNet removed the first two fully connected layers that accounted for a substantial proportion of AlexNet's parameters and replaced them with global average pooling, thereby reducing the overall parameter count to approximately one-twelfth that of AlexNet. To facilitate the training of deeper networks, auxiliary classifiers were inserted into intermediate layers, providing additional supervision signals that alleviated the vanishing gradient problem and improved training stability and convergence efficiency. By contrast, the VGG network, developed by the Visual Geometry Group (VGG) at the University of Oxford, abandoned the large 11×11 and 5×5 convolutional kernels employed in AlexNet and instead adopted multiple stacked 3×3 convolutional kernels to enlarge the effective receptive field while maintaining computational efficiency. In addition, VGG eliminated the Local Response Normalization (LRN) operation used in AlexNet, resulting in a simpler and more unified network architecture. In general, deeper neural networks are capable of extracting higher-level and more abstract feature representations. However, as network depth and parameter counts increase, two major challenges emerge. First, the backpropagation process becomes increasingly susceptible to vanishing or exploding gradients, which may significantly slow convergence or even cause training failure. Second, the enhanced fitting capability of large-scale networks may capture noise within the training data, thereby increasing the risk of overfitting and degrading generalization performance on previously unseen data distributions. To address these issues, Kaiming He and colleagues from Microsoft Research Asia proposed the Residual Network (ResNet) in 2015, which won the ILSVRC championship. ResNet introduced the innovative concept of residual learning through shortcut connections, effectively overcoming optimization difficulties associated with very deep neural networks. Its core idea is to establish identity mappings that allow input features to bypass intermediate layers and be directly added to the output, thereby preserving original information and facilitating gradient propagation during backpropagation. Furthermore, ResNet systematically incorporated Batch Normalization (BN) throughout the network architecture, which further alleviated vanishing and exploding gradient problems and improved training stability. Today, residual connections have become one of the fundamental architectural components of modern large-scale deep neural networks and are widely adopted in state-of-the-art deep learning models.

作为同样隶属于机器学习体系的训练逻辑，如图1.7所示的强化学习标准架构包含着两个核心模块（智能体、环境）以及三种变量类型（状态、动作、奖励）。其基本范式流程可以描述为：智能体在当前策略的引导下，根据环境的状态选择相应动作；当环境执行该动作后，在转移到下一个状态的同时生成相应的奖励值；智能体将依据所产生的经验样本计算损失与梯度后更新当前策略。基于上述流程不断地试错探索当前状态空间对应的最优策略，当总累计奖励稳定收敛于最大值，意味着智能体掌握了在当前环境的最优策略。经过多年的发展归纳，强化学习的算法体系可根据样本特征划分为以下三类：基于动态规划（Dynamic Programming, DP）、基于蒙特卡洛（Monte Carlo, MC）与基于时序差分（Temporal Difference, TD）。同时，还可以依据离线学习Offline Learning/在线学习Online Learning、基于模型Model-based/免模型Model-free以及固定性策略On-Policy/非固定性策略Off-Policy这三类指标进一步细分。

As a learning paradigm within the broader machine learning framework, the standard reinforcement learning architecture, as illustrated in Figure 1.7, consists of two core components—the agent and the environment—as well as three fundamental types of variables: state, action, and reward. The basic interaction process can be described as follows: the agent selects an action based on the current policy and the observed state of the environment. After the environment executes the action, it transitions to a new state and simultaneously generates a corresponding reward signal. The agent then updates its current policy by computing loss functions and gradients based on the collected experience samples. Through continuous trial-and-error interactions within the state space, the agent progressively improves its policy. When the cumulative reward converges to a stable maximum, the agent is considered to have learned an optimal policy for the given environment. Over the course of its development, reinforcement learning algorithms can be broadly categorized into three major classes based on the underlying learning mechanism: Dynamic Programming (DP), Monte Carlo (MC), and Temporal Difference (TD) methods. In addition, reinforcement learning methods can be further classified along multiple dimensions, including Offline Learning versus Online Learning, Model-based versus Model-free approaches, and On-policy versus Off-policy strategies.

动态规划DP算法本身是在1954年由Richard Bellman提出，旨在将复杂的大规模问题分解为子问题后，通过将子问题的解重新组合并构造原问题的最终解。就基于模型/离线学习的动态规划型强化学习而言，掌握有限环境的状态转移矩阵（State Transition Matrix, STM）后将采集所有马尔科夫决策过程（Markov Decision Process, MDP）的样本轨迹，并且在当前阶段实现对策略的迭代更新。对于免模型/离线学习的蒙特卡洛型强化学习，核心的蒙特卡洛法由Stanislaw Ulam于1949年提出，致力于在面对无法准确建模的环境特征时，通过大量的数据采样表征其内部的变化规律。1987年，来自美国加利福尼亚大学的Ronald J. Williams提出了基于蒙特卡洛法的强化学习算法REINFORCE，并引入了梯度下降法更新策略。然而，面对现实世界中大量的免模型任务时，动态规划DP与蒙特卡洛MC都难以实现满意的学习效率。随后，1988年加拿大阿尔伯塔大学的“现代强化学习之父”Richard S. Sutton提出了基于时序差分TD的强化学习算法类型，隶属于免模型/在线学习的类别。其中，作为固定性策略的SARSA由Gavin. Adrian. Rummery于1994年提出，属于非固定性策略的Q-Learning算法则源自于1989年Christopher J. Watkins博士论文中提出多步时序差分法。1998年，Richard S. Sutton发表的《Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction》被誉为强化学习的“圣经级”教科书。1999年，Sutton针对策略梯度与函数逼近的结合进行系统性分析，形成了现代强化学习体系中以策略优化与函数逼近为基础的算法原理。此外，部分经典架构也于二十一世纪初被相继提出，如Andrew Ng和Stuart Russell于2000年提出逆向强化学习（Inverse RL, IRL），基于数据样本反向推断潜在奖励函数；2007年，法国里尔大学Rémi Coulom提出蒙特卡洛搜索树（Monte Carlo Tree Search, MCST）将对后续围棋智能体AlphaGo的诞生发挥关键作用。鉴于上述传统强化学习的经典案例，也为后续深度强化学习算法的发展奠定了坚实的理论体系与算法基础。然而，强化学习的早期发展除了同样受到数据与算力等方面的限制外，算法本身也存在着稳定性与可靠性的缺陷。由于在强化学习的算法架构中依赖于迭代试错，因此存在陷入局部最优或难以稳定收敛等问题。此外，基于离散表格Tabular-based的传统强化学习算法也存在难以解决维度灾难与离散误差的局限性问题。综上，由图1.4所示发展时间轴可知，强化学习算法体系也经历了长达十余年的沉寂期。直到2010年，Demis Hassabis等人在英国伦敦创建了人工智能团队DeepMind，由Volodymyr Mnih等人于2013年首次提出了深度强化学习概念与深度值网络（Deep Q-Network, DQN）算法。随后，该团队于2014年被谷歌公司收购并于2023年与Google Brain合并为Google DeepMind。

Dynamic Programming (DP) was originally proposed by Richard Bellman in 1954 as a general framework for solving complex large-scale problems by decomposing them into subproblems and reconstructing the global solution from the solutions of these subproblems. In reinforcement learning, DP-based methods typically assume a known environment model. Given the state transition matrix (State Transition Matrix, STM), all trajectories of a Markov Decision Process (MDP) can be enumerated or evaluated, enabling iterative policy improvement in a model-based and offline manner. In contrast, Monte Carlo (MC) methods, which are also typically categorized as model-free and offline approaches, were first introduced by Stanislaw Ulam in 1949. MC methods aim to approximate system dynamics through extensive random sampling when an accurate analytical model of the environment is unavailable. In 1987, Ronald J. Williams from the University of California proposed the REINFORCE algorithm based on Monte Carlo sampling, introducing gradient-based policy optimization for reinforcement learning. However, in many real-world model-free tasks, both DP and MC methods suffer from inefficiencies in learning. To address these limitations, Temporal Difference (TD) learning was proposed by Richard S. Sutton in 1988 at the University of Alberta. TD methods belong to the class of model-free and online learning algorithms, combining ideas from both DP and MC by bootstrapping value estimates. Within TD-based methods, SARSA, introduced by Gavin A. Rummery in 1994, is a representative on-policy algorithm, while Q-Learning, derived from the multi-step TD framework introduced in Christopher J. Watkins’ doctoral dissertation in 1989, is a representative off-policy algorithm. In 1998, Richard S. Sutton published the influential book *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction*, which is widely regarded as the “bible” of reinforcement learning. In 1999, Sutton further provided a systematic analysis of the integration of policy gradient methods and function approximation, laying the foundation for modern reinforcement learning frameworks based on policy optimization and value function approximation. In addition, several classical reinforcement learning paradigms were proposed in the early 21st century. Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL), introduced by Andrew Ng and Stuart Russell in 2000, aims to infer the underlying reward function from observed behavior data. In 2007, Rémi Coulom proposed Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), which later played a crucial role in the development of game-playing systems such as AlphaGo. Despite these advances, early reinforcement learning methods were still constrained by data scarcity and limited computational resources. Moreover, algorithmic stability and reliability remained major challenges. Due to the reliance on iterative trial-and-error learning, reinforcement learning algorithms are prone to local optima and unstable convergence. In addition, traditional tabular-based methods suffer from the curse of dimensionality and discretization limitations, making them unsuitable for high-dimensional continuous problems. As illustrated in the development timeline (Figure 1.4), reinforcement learning experienced a relatively long period of stagnation. This situation changed in 2010 with the establishment of DeepMind in London by Demis Hassabis and colleagues. In 2013, Volodymyr Mnih and collaborators first introduced the concept of Deep Reinforcement Learning and proposed the Deep Q-Network (DQN) algorithm. Subsequently, DeepMind was acquired by Google in 2014 and later merged with Google Brain in 2023 to form Google DeepMind.

接下来，以2015年为发展元年，正式迎来深度强化学习的热潮。在算法层面，更多类型的深度强化学习算法以及改进措施被相继提出，如Timothy P. Lillicrap等人提出的深度确定性策略梯度（Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient, DDPG），Tom Schaul提出的优先经验回放（Prioritized Experience Replay, PER），John Schulman提出的置信域策略优化（Trust Region Policy Optimization, TRPO），Volodymyr Mnih提出的异步优势演员-评论家（Asynchronous Advantage Actor-Critic, A3C），John Schulman提出的近端策略优化（Proximal Policy Optimization, PPO），Tuomas Haarnoja提出的软演员-评论家（Soft Actor-Critic, SAC）与Scott Fujimoto提出的双延迟深度确定性策略梯度（Twin Delayed DDPG, TD3）。上述内容在图1.4所示发展时间轴内均有所列举，并且这些也是近年来得到广泛应用的代表性算法。从算法架构、探索机制、训练稳定性与收敛效果方面综合对比，就个人经验而言，近端策略优化PPO与软演员-评论家SAC是目前兼具实用性与稳定性的可选方案。对于如前所述由国产大语言模型DeepSeek-R1提出的GRPO算法而言，相比之下也是以近端策略优化PPO算法为基础，在面向大语言模型LLM任务经过适配性调整后提出的训练逻辑，而主要差异就在于优势函数与KL散度的定义。

Starting from 2015, the field of deep reinforcement learning entered a period of rapid development. At the algorithmic level, a series of representative methods and improvements were successively proposed, significantly advancing both theoretical research and practical applications. Notable examples include Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG) proposed by Timothy P. Lillicrap et al., Prioritized Experience Replay (PER) introduced by Tom Schaul, Trust Region Policy Optimization (TRPO) proposed by John Schulman, Asynchronous Advantage Actor-Critic (A3C) introduced by Volodymyr Mnih et al., Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) proposed by John Schulman, Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) proposed by Tuomas Haarnoja, and Twin Delayed DDPG (TD3) proposed by Scott Fujimoto. These methods are all illustrated within the development timeline shown in Figure 1.4, and they collectively represent the most widely adopted deep reinforcement learning algorithms in recent years. From a comprehensive perspective considering algorithmic architecture, exploration strategy, training stability, and convergence performance, Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) and Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) are generally regarded as two of the most practical and stable choices for a wide range of applications. Regarding the Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) method proposed in the DeepSeek-R1 framework developed by DeepSeek, it can be interpreted as an extension built upon the PPO framework. After being adapted for large language model (LLM) training scenarios, its primary distinction lies in the formulation of the advantage function and the treatment of the Kullback–Leibler (KL) divergence constraint. In essence, GRPO modifies the optimization objective to better suit large-scale language model alignment, while maintaining the core policy optimization paradigm established by PPO.

由此开始，按照时间轴顺序介绍深度强化学习算法的代表性成果，致力于为读者建立面向深度强化学习算法未来的发展信心，并且其核心的作用及优势在于：通过合理的训练方案，能够让智能体始终向着更优甚至最优的方向持续突破。

From this point onward, representative achievements in deep reinforcement learning are introduced in chronological order, with the aim of providing readers with a clear understanding of the field’s evolutionary trajectory and strengthening confidence in its future development. The core idea and key advantage of deep reinforcement learning lie in its ability, through properly designed training schemes, to continuously guide the agent toward progressively better and potentially optimal policies. By leveraging large-scale interaction data and iterative optimization mechanisms, the agent is capable of sustained performance improvement within complex and dynamic environments.

首先，2015年，经过改进后的深度值网络DQN算法由于在Atari 2600平台的像素游戏中展现出人类级别的控制效果，因此以《Human-level control through deep reinforcement learning》为题的论文成果成为第7540期Nature封面。到2016年，DeepMind团队推出的围棋智能体AlphaGo引起了全球范围轰动，相应的论文成果《Mastering the game of Go with deep neural networks and tree search》是第7587期Nature封面。随后，通过持续的模型迭代与自我对弈，改进版AlphaGo Zero与AlphaZero取得了更加优秀的战绩。该款围棋智能体先后击败了欧洲冠军樊麾、世界冠军李世石与世界第一柯洁，标志着基于深度强化学习与蒙特卡洛搜索树的人工智能系统在复杂博弈任务中全面超越人类的顶尖水平。2019年，同样来自于DeepMind团队的Oriol Vinyals等人提出了AlphaStar智能体。该模型以经典且热门的电子竞技项目《星际争霸II》为任务背景，不仅掌握了所有种族的策略体系，而且在天梯对战中达到了宗师Grandmaster级别。2020年，DeepMind团队的Julian Schrittwieser提出了MuZero，联合学习了隐式环境模型、策略函数与价值函数，协同掌握Atari 2600、围棋、国际象棋与日本将棋等策略。接下来，作为最重量级的研究成果，在2021年由DeepMind团队John Jumper等人发布了根据氨基酸序列精准预测蛋白质三维结构的AlphaFold，并且于2022年在第7873期Nature封面发表了名为《Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold》论文成果，彻底革新了结构生物学的发展态势与进程。因此，瑞典皇家科学院也将2024年的诺贝尔化学奖颁发给DeepMind的CEO Demis Hassabis与项目负责人John Jumper。面向车辆工程与机器人领域，Sony AI于2022年在第7897期的Nature封面发布了战胜人类冠军级车手的赛车竞速智能体GT Sophy；瑞士苏黎世大学于2023年在7976期Nature封面发布了无人机竞速智能体Swift，应用于真实的物理环境，并且同样达到战胜人类冠军的操控水平。值得关注的是，上述研究均采用常规或改进型全连接神经网络与深度强化学习算法，而核心贡献在于训练方案的创新与控制效果的突破。国内范围，清华大学Shuo Feng于2023年7953期Nature封面发表了《Dense Reinforcement Learning for Safety Validation of Autonomous Vehicles》，提出了基于密集强化学习的自动驾驶安全测试方法。通过删除次要状态并且连接关键状态，实现了可编辑的马尔科夫连续决策过程，在应对稀疏灾难与维度灾难的角度解决了长尾稀疏性极端场景的生成问题。

Starting in 2015, the development of deep reinforcement learning entered a phase of rapid and milestone-driven progress. In that year, the improved Deep Q-Network (DQN) demonstrated human-level performance on Atari 2600 pixel-based games. The corresponding paper, *Human-level control through deep reinforcement learning*, was published in *Nature* (Issue 7540), marking one of the earliest landmark achievements in the field. In 2016, the AlphaGo system developed by the DeepMind team attracted worldwide attention. Its associated paper, *Mastering the game of Go with deep neural networks and tree search*, was published in *Nature* (Issue 7587). Subsequently, through continuous self-play and iterative optimization, the improved versions AlphaGo Zero and AlphaZero achieved even stronger performance. These systems defeated top-level human players, including European champion Fan Hui, world champion Lee Sedol, and world number one Ke Jie, demonstrating that deep reinforcement learning combined with Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) had reached a level surpassing elite human performance in complex strategic games. In 2019, DeepMind introduced AlphaStar (Oriol Vinyals et al.), a reinforcement learning agent designed for the real-time strategy game *StarCraft II*. The system successfully mastered multi-race strategies and achieved Grandmaster-level performance in competitive ladder matches. In 2020, DeepMind further proposed MuZero (Julian Schrittwieser et al.), which jointly learned an implicit environment model, policy function, and value function, achieving strong performance across Atari 2600 games, chess, shogi, and Go without requiring explicit knowledge of game dynamics. In 2021, another major breakthrough was achieved with AlphaFold, developed by John Jumper and colleagues at DeepMind, which accurately predicted three-dimensional protein structures from amino acid sequences. The corresponding paper, *Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold*, was published in *Nature* (Issue 7873) in 2022. This work fundamentally transformed the field of structural biology and its research paradigm. In recognition of its impact, the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences awarded the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry to Demis Hassabis and John Jumper. In the fields of robotics and vehicle engineering, Sony AI released GT Sophy in 2022, featured on the cover of *Nature* (Issue 7897), demonstrating human-level and beyond-human performance in competitive racing tasks. In 2023, researchers from ETH Zurich presented Swift, a drone racing agent published in *Nature* (Issue 7976), which achieved expert-level performance in real-world physical environments. It is noteworthy that most of these studies rely on standard or improved fully connected neural networks combined with deep reinforcement learning frameworks. Their core contributions primarily lie in innovations in training strategies and significant improvements in control performance rather than radical changes in network architecture. In China, Tsinghua University’s Feng Shuo et al. published *Dense Reinforcement Learning for Safety Validation of Autonomous Vehicles* in *Nature* (Issue 7953) in 2023. The work proposed a dense reinforcement learning framework for safety testing of autonomous driving systems. By removing redundant states and connecting critical states, an editable Markov decision process was constructed, effectively addressing challenges related to sparse reward signals, rare event sampling, and high-dimensional long-tail scenarios in safety-critical environments.
